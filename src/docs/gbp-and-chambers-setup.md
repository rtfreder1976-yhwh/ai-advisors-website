### Step 9: Review Generation Strategy

**How to Ask for Reviews:**

1. After every successful engagement, send this text/email:

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks again for working with us on [project]. If you found value in our AI consulting, would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It helps other local businesses find us.

Here's the direct link: [Your Google Review Link]

Just a sentence or two about your experience is perfect. Thanks so much!

- Todd
```

2. **Get Your Review Link:**
   - Go to Google Business Profile
   - Click "Get more reviews"
   - Copy the short link

**Review Keywords to Encourage:**
Ask satisfied clients to mention:
- Location (Huntsville, Florence, Shoals)
- Service type (AI training, automation, consulting)
- Results (time saved, leads captured, etc.)

Example prompt: "If you could mention that we helped your [industry] business in [city], that would be really helpful for other local businesses searching for AI help."

## PART 2: CHAMBER OF COMMERCE DIRECTORIES

### Shoals Chamber of Commerce

**Website:** https://shoalschamber.com

**Steps:**
1. Go to: https://shoalschamber.com/join/
2. Choose membership level (likely "Professional" or "Small Business")
3. Fill out application with business details

**Membership Benefits:**
- Directory listing
- Networking events
- "Lunch & Learn" speaking opportunities (PITCH TO SPEAK ABOUT AI)
- Member-to-member referrals

**Cost:** Typically $200-400/year depending on tier

**Priority: HIGH** - This is your home market

---

### Huntsville/Madison County Chamber

**Website:** https://hsvchamber.org

**Steps:**
1. Go to: https://hsvchamber.org/join/
2. Select membership level
3. Complete application

**Key Benefits:**
- Largest chamber in North Alabama
- Defense contractor connections
- Research Park business network
- Directory visibility for "Huntsville AI" searches

**Cost:** Typically $300-600/year

**Priority: HIGH** - Critical for Huntsville market penetration

## PART 3: OTHER DIRECTORY SUBMISSIONS

### High Priority (Free)

**1. Yelp Business**
- https://biz.yelp.com
- Claim/create business listing
- Category: "Business Consulting"
- Add photos, services, hours

**2. Better Business Bureau**
- https://www.bbb.org/get-accredited
- Consider accreditation for trust signal
- Even free listing adds credibility

**3. Alignable**
- https://www.alignable.com
- B2B networking platform
- Very active in local business communities

**4. LinkedIn Company Page**
- Create/optimize company page
- Add services, locations
- Post weekly content

**5. Apple Maps**
- https://mapsconnect.apple.com
- Add business listing
- Often overlooked but valuable

**6. Bing Places**
- https://www.bingplaces.com
- Import from Google or create new
- Free, takes 10 minutes
