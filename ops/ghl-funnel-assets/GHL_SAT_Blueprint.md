## 3. Technical Setup in GoHighLevel

### The Two-Step Order Form
*   **Step 1:** First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, Business Name.
*   **Step 2:** Payment (Stripe).
*   **Order Bump:** "Add 50 Custom Citations for $97? (Normally $297)"
*   **Guarantee Placement:** Place the "Unbeatable Guarantee" logo and text directly under the payment button.

### Workflow: Cart Abandonment
1. **Trigger:** Order Form Submitted (Step 1).
2. **Filter:** Step 2 (Payment) = Incomplete.
3. **Wait:** 15 Minutes.
4. **Action (SMS):** "Hey [FirstName], I saw you checking out the Voice Search Protocol. Did the connection drop or did you have a quick question? I'm holding your spot for the audit. - [YourName]"

### Workflow: Post-Purchase Activation
1. **Trigger:** Order Submitted (Global Product).
2. **Action (Internal):** Tag contact [Client: Voice Search].
3. **Action (SMS):** "Congrats [FirstName]! You're in. My team is already starting the Apple Maps forensic audit. Check your email for next steps."
4. **Action (Email):** Automated onboarding form request.
5. **Action (Document):** Auto-send Service Agreement via GHL Documents.
